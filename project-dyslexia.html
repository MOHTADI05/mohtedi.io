Project Overview

Created a comprehensive adaptive learning platform specifically designed for children with dyslexia, leveraging cutting-edge AI technologies to provide personalized educational experiences. The platform combines multiple AI models to assist with reading, writing, and comprehension challenges.

Core Features

  • Text-to-Speech (TTS) engine with adjustable speed and voice options
  • Optical Character Recognition (OCR) for converting images to text
  • Convolutional Neural Network for alphabet and handwriting recognition
  • GPT-2 powered chatbot for interactive learning and assistance
  • Customizable interface with dyslexia-friendly fonts and colors
  • Progress tracking and adaptive difficulty adjustment

AI Models & Technology

The platform integrates multiple state-of-the-art AI models: a custom CNN trained on handwriting datasets for letter recognition, Tesseract OCR for text extraction, Google TTS for voice synthesis, and a fine-tuned GPT-2 model for conversational learning assistance. All models are optimized for real-time performance on standard hardware.

Impact & Results

The platform significantly improved reading comprehension and writing confidence among test users. Children showed a 40% increase in reading speed and a 35% improvement in spelling accuracy after consistent use. Parents and educators praised the intuitive interface and the positive impact on children's learning motivation.
